Welcome to UNGOV

Government policy needs a fresh perspective. It's time to put your ideas forward.

 

We’re making a few changes behind the scenes, please bear with us!

How it works

Ideas

You tell us how government policy can be improved. We keep your identity a secret, so you can be as radical as possible.

Submit a new idea

Validation

Anonymous proposals enter our library for the community to review. Anyone can post a comment or edit a proposal to make it better.

Review existing ideas

Change

We identify the strongest proposals, and introduce them to people and organisations with the power to make change happen.

Get in touch

About

Government policy matters, but modern government is tired. We're here to help, by making it easier to share policy ideas and work together on how things can be improved.
